About Havens Cove

Dedicated to helping individuals rebuild their lives through recovery

Our Story

Founded in 2012, Havens Cove Rehabilitation Centre began as a small treatment facility with a clear mission—to help individuals overcome addiction and rebuild their lives. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted centre offering comprehensive recovery programs, professional medical care, and emotional support.
