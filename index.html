About Our Centre

Havens Cove Rehabilitation Centre provides professional and compassionate care for individuals struggling with substance abuse. Our specialists deliver structured, evidence-based programs focused on long-term recovery.

💬

Individual and group counselling sessions

🧠

Behavioural therapy and mental health support

💊

Medication-assisted treatment when required

Family support and education programs
